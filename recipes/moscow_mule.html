Moscow Mule

Description

The tasty Moscow Mule, a cocktail made of Vodka, Lime and Gingerbeer

Ingredients

  • 55ml Vodka
  • 120ml Gingerbeer
  • 1/2 Teespoon Angosture Bitter
  • 1/2 Lime in 2 parts
  • Crushed ice

Steps

  1. Fill a copper mug with crushed ice.
  2. Pour the vodka in the mug.
  3. Squeeze 2 lime parts into the mug and keep them.
  4. Add the angostura bitters to the mug.
  5. Fill the mug with gingerbeer and give it a stir. Use the lime parts as garnish.
