Blue Lagoon

Description

The tasty Blue Lagoon, a tropical cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 40ml Blue Curacao
  • 20ml Vodka
  • Lemonade
  • Icecubes
  • Orange slice or lime part as garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with icecubes.
  2. Pour the Blue Curacao into the glass.
  3. Add the fresh lime juice.
  4. Fill the glass with lemonade
  5. Stir gently
  6. Add garnish. Cheers!
