Cuba Libre

Description

The tasty Cuba Libre, a cocktail made of Rum and coca-cola

Ingredients

  • 50ml dark rum
  • 100ml cold coca-cola
  • 2 lime parts
  • Icecubes

Steps

  1. Fill a glass with icecubes.
  2. Squeeze the lime into the glass.
  3. Add 50ml of dark rum.
  4. Fill the glass with 100ml icecold coca-cola and give it a stir.
